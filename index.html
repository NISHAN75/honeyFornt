The taste of home

Opening in late 2024, HoneyFront is Albany’s kitchen table, where every meal is a celebration of our city’s heritage of hospitality and culinary expertise. Located off South Front Street, inside the lobby of the Albany Hilton, we invite you to savor soulfully crafted dishes that reflect our hometown’s warmth and richness.

a little about

the chef

Dominic Simpson leads as the newly appointed Director of Culinary, who brings over 25 years of exceptional leadership as an Executive Chef. Hailing from Atlanta, Georgia, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to HoneyFront. His culinary journey began under the tutelage of renowned Chef Paul Albrecht and Chef Joel Antuens, where he developed a deep understanding of the culinary arts. Throughout his illustrious career, Chef Simpson has held the Executive Chef position at the Crowne Plaza, N.A./S.C., showcasing he exceptional ability to manage culinary operations, create innovative menus, and lead a team of talented chefs. Come experience “the taste of home” with Chef Simpson!
